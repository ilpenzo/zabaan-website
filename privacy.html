Zabaan Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Zabaan is designed with your child's privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No accounts or sign-ups required.
  • No personal data collected.
  • No analytics or tracking.
  • No third-party advertising.

Microphone usage

Zabaan uses the device microphone solely for pronunciation practice exercises. Audio is sent to Google Cloud Speech-to-Text for evaluation and is not stored by Zabaan or Google after processing. No audio recordings are saved on the device or transmitted elsewhere.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

Zabaan is designed for children ages 8–12. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to protect, share, or delete.

Data stored on device

Lesson progress and settings are stored locally on the device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.

In-app purchases

Zabaan offers optional in-app purchases to unlock additional levels. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple or Google through their respective app stores. Zabaan does not receive or store any payment information.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@zabaanapp.com.
