How Zabaan Works

  • 🔊

    Audio-first learning

    Learn by listening and speaking — the way language is naturally acquired.

  • 🎙

    Pronunciation feedback

    Speech recognition listens to your child and helps them improve their accent.

  • 👨‍👩‍👧

    Conversations with family

    Practice with characters like Mamani (Grandma) and Amoo Reza (Uncle) in real-life scenarios.

  • 🔄

    Spaced repetition

    Lessons are timed to help your child remember what they learn — for good.

  • 📶

    Works offline

    Download lessons and practice anywhere, no internet needed.

  • 🔒

    Private and safe

    No ads, no accounts, no data collection. Built for kids, trusted by parents.
